**User Journey to Learn About Rider's Services**

As a potential customer exploring logistics options in Pakistan,
I gather detailed information about Rider's services and delivery options,
so that I can make an informed decision on whether to use their logistics services for my business needs.

When I click on the About link and navigate to the About Us page, then click on the Contact link for more information, and further investigate the FAQ about estimated delivery time
Then I should see the information about Rider's service offerings and the estimated delivery times for city orders clearly outlined, ensuring I understand their logistics solutions.
