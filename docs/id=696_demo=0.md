# Task

**Explore Federal Contracts and Resources**

As a government contractor,
I explore available federal contract opportunities and access relevant resources to aid in my bidding process,
so that I can effectively identify and navigate through government contracts that align with my business goals.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the Sweetspot homepage and interested in government contracting
When I click on the 'Find Federal Contracts' link and then click on the 'Resources' button
Then I should be taken to the Federal Contract Search page and see a list of relevant federal contract opportunities, followed by an expanded resource menu with links to the Blog and GovCon Glossary.
