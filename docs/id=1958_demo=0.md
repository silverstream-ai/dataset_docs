# Task

**User Exploration on Payflow Website**

As a potential customer,
I learn about the products and opportunities offered by Payflow,
so that I can determine how Payflow can meet my needs and possibly align with my career goals.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the Payflow homepage
When I click on the 'Nuestros productos' button, then the 'Media' button, and finally the 'Sobre nosotros y empleo' link
Then I should see information about Payflows products, media offerings, and a detailed overview of the company's mission, values, and job opportunities.
