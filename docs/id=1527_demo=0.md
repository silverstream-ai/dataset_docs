# Task

**User Journey on AntWalk's Website**

As a prospective customer,
I understand the benefits of using AntWalk's platform and see real-world results of their solutions,
so that I can make an informed decision about whether to engage with AntWalk for my organization's training needs.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the AntWalk homepage
When I have clicked the 'Why AntWalk?' link and then the 'Success Stories' link
Then I should be able to view information about the capabilities of AntWalk's services and access testimonials and case studies demonstrating the impact of their solutions.
