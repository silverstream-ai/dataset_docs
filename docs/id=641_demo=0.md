# Task

**Explore Accend Website Services and Insights**

As a potential client interested in Accend's services,
I learn more about the company and its offerings,
so that I can make an informed decision about engaging their services.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the Accend homepage
When I click on the About link and then explore the Solutions and Blog sections
Then I should gain insights into Accends mission and offerings, as well as access recent articles that inform my understanding of the industry.
