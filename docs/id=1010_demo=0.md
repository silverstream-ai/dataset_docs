# Task

**User Story Exploring Trackstars Solutions**

As a business owner,
I explore the different solutions and pricing options offered by Trackstar,
so that I can understand how Trackstar's services can support my business needs efficiently.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the Trackstar homepage
When I click on the Solutions button and select Commerce Enablement and then the Pricing link
Then I should see the Commerce Enablement section showcasing its features and afterward view the Pricing page with detailed pricing information.
