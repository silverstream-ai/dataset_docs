# Task

**User Story for Chari Navigation**

As a business owner,
I read about the company's background and customer experiences,
so that I can understand how Chari can benefit my business operations.

**Success definition:** Given I am on the Chari homepage, having accessed the website
When I click on the 'A PROPOS' and 'TÉMOIGNAGES' links in the navigation
Then I should see detailed information about the company followed by a section displaying customer testimonials that highlight their experiences with Chari.
